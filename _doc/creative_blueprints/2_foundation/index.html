The Pitch

From Problem to Play

The Problem

No quick game
without the friction

When two people want a spontaneous game, every option demands a download, an account, or a phone. A classic two-minute game becomes a five-minute setup chore.

Apps require installs and logins
Ads and onboarding kill the moment
Spontaneity lost before the first move
The Solution

Open browser.
Start playing.

Tic-Tac-Toe loads instantly in any browser. No accounts, no downloads — a clean 3×3 grid, two players, and one click to reset for the next round.

Zero setup — loads in milliseconds
No accounts, no ads, no friction
Infinite replays — one click to restart
